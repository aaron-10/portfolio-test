Some projects

3D printed rotary subwoofer

A rotary subwoofer, which creates low frequencies by modulating the blade pitch of a fan. By switching the direction of airflow back and forth very quickly it can generate sound waves.

Adaptive controller for OmniMorph

Adaptive controller design for a new type of UAV with 8 tilting rotors. The controller aims to use the full potential in each configuration of the rotors.

Roller coaster track automation

Python script to automate generating rail and spine splines based on an input spline and banking parameters. Also working on a script to automate Fusion 360 to generate the 3D model.

Model rollercoaster

3D printed model roller coaster I have been experimenting with for some time. Long term work in progress...

Solar panel fabrication

For Solar Boat Twente I worked on fabricating solar panels by hand. Soldering the cells, laminating panels and installation on the Solar Boat. Really interesting challenge.

60V NMC battery pack

Water cooled 14s NCM lithium battery pack for Solar Boat Twente.

60V LFP battery pack

Water cooled 16s LeFePO4 lithium battery pack for Solar Boat Twente.

Solar Boat visuals

3D graphics for the boat reveal of Solar Boat Twente. Created a detailed textured model in Blender and rendered some animations to support the story in the technical presentation.

Racing drone

Racing quadcoter built around a 5-inch carbon fibre frame. It has a camera and a video transmitter, so you can fly it with FPV goggles. It reaches top speeds of 90km/h, quite scary.

Launchpad

Small launchpad to easily control things on a PC, such as hotkeys, launching programs, or muting the microphone. It has a touch sensor on the side to switch between button profiles.

Ceiling square lamp

A big wooden hanging wooden square with adressable LED strips. I can be controlled via WiFi using the ArtNet protocol, so it can easily be synchronised with other lights.

Camera Jib

A 4 meter long camera crane for stably recording events on stage. Tilt is maintained passively while the pan is actively controlled by a motor.
